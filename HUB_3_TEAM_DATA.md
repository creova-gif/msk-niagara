## Project Focus Areas

### Project #1: Identity & Belonging within Afro Descendants
**Research Focus:**
- Afro-descendant community identity in Niagara Region
- Belonging and integration experiences
- Community building and cultural preservation
- Social connections and support networks

**Key Partners:**
- SOFIFRAN (Francophone services)
- Welland Heritage Council & Multicultural Centre
- TOES Niagara
- Centre canadien pour L'unite de la famille

---

### Project #2: Migrant Farmworkers in Community
**Research Focus:**
- Seasonal agricultural worker experiences
- Community integration and belonging
- Worker rights and advocacy
- Faith-based support systems
- Social isolation and connection

**Key Partners:**
- Migrant Farm Workers Project: St. Alban's Anglican Church
- Community Potential - Niagara Connects

---

### Project #3: ICT Use & SGD Newcomers
**Research Focus:**
- Information and Communication Technology (ICT) access and use
- Sexual and Gender Diverse (SGD) newcomer experiences
- Digital inclusion and literacy
- LGBTQ+ immigrant and refugee support
- Technology as a tool for community connection

**Key Partners:**
- Niagara Local Immigration Partnership
- Community Potential - Niagara Connects
- Le Réseau en Immigration Francophone
