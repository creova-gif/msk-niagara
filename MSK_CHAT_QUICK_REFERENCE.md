## 🎨 Using MSK Chat

### Opening the Chat
1. Click the **dark red chat button** in the bottom-right corner
2. Chat window opens with welcome message
3. Choose a **quick reply** or type your own question

### Asking Questions
1. **Type naturally** - MSK Chat understands conversational language
2. **Use keywords** - Include relevant terms from your topic
3. **Be specific** - More detail = better answers
4. **Try different phrasings** - If you don't get what you need, rephrase

### Quick Replies
After the welcome message, you'll see **4 quick topic buttons**:
- **Research Hubs** / Pôles de recherche
- **Our Mission** / Notre mission
- **Get Involved** / Participer
- **Team & Partners** / Équipe & Partenaires

Click any button for instant answers!

### Language Support
- Chat responds in your **selected website language**
- Switch language using the **language toggle** in the header
- All responses are **professionally translated**

### Closing the Chat
- Click the **X button** in the chat window header
- Or click the **dark red chat button** again
- Your conversation is cleared when you close (privacy)

## ⚡ Pro Tips

### Get Better Answers
1. **Ask one question at a time** for clearer responses
2. **Use complete sentences** when possible
3. **Include context** - "I'm a student researcher interested in..."
4. **Follow up** - Ask for more details if needed

### Navigation
- Responses often mention **website pages** to visit
- Use the **main navigation** to explore further
- Chat provides **actionable next steps**

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### "I didn't understand your question"
✅ **Try these:**
- Rephrase using keywords from topic areas
- Ask about specific topics (hubs, team, partners)
- Use a quick reply button
- Check example questions above

### "Response isn't detailed enough"
✅ **Try these:**
- Ask for more details: "Tell me more about..."
- Ask about specific aspects: "What's the focus of...?"
- Navigate to the relevant website page for full details

### "Chat isn't opening"
✅ **Check:**
- Click the dark red button in bottom-right
- Refresh the page if needed
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled

## 🎯 What MSK Chat Won't Do

MSK Chat is informational only. It **cannot**:
- ❌ Schedule meetings or events
- ❌ Submit partnership applications
- ❌ Provide personal contact details
- ❌ Make decisions or commitments
- ❌ Access your personal information
- ❌ Remember previous conversations (after closing)

**For official inquiries**, use the website contact page or attend events!
