- **Research Area:** Access to sport and recreational programs
- **Community Impact:** Inclusive recreation programming

## Research Methodology

### Community-Based Participatory Research (CBPR)
- Community partners co-lead research design
- Children and families as active participants
- Action-oriented research for real-world impact

### Multilingual Approach
- Research conducted in English, French, Spanish
- SOFIFRAN partnership ensures Francophone inclusion
- Culturally adapted research materials

### Youth & Family Centered
- Centering children's voices and experiences
- Family systems approach
- Age-appropriate research methods
